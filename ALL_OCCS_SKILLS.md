# Complete OCC Skills List - Palladium Fantasy (1994, 2nd Edition Core)

This document lists all **core** Occupational Character Classes (OCCs) from the 1994 2nd Edition rulebook with their OCC Skills, Elective Skills, and Secondary Skills.

**Note:** Optional OCCs (Peasant, Merchant, Noble, Scholar, Squire) and expansion OCCs (Gladiator, Pirate, Sailor, Monk, Alchemist) have been removed to match the core rulebook.

## CORE SECONDARY SKILLS POOL

All OCCs can choose from this pool for their Secondary Skills (subject to restrictions). These skills match the 1994 2nd Edition rulebook:

- Carpentry
- Cook
- Dance
- Demon and Devil Lore
- Faerie Lore
- Imitate Voices
- Mathematics
- Paint
- Plant/Farm Lore
- Play String Instruments
- Play Wind Instruments
- Preserve Food
- Racial Histories
- Read/Write Additional Language
- Recognize Poison
- Recognize Precious Metals/Stones
- Recognize Weapon Quality
- Religious Doctrine
- Sailing
- Sense of Direction
- Sing
- Speak Additional Language
- Swim
- Tailor
- Ventriloquism

---

**Note:** Secondary Skills typically have penalties compared to OCC or Elective Skills (e.g., -10% to -15% for certain skills). Check individual skill descriptions for specific penalties.

**Note on Optional OCCs:**

The following optional OCCs are included (available at G.M.'s discretion per the core rulebook):

- Peasant, Squire, Scholar, Merchant, Noble, Alchemist

**Removed Non-Core Elements:**

- OCCs: Illusionist, Gladiator, Pirate, Sailor, Monk
- Skills: W.P. Net, W.P. Garrote, W.P. Cutlass, W.P. Harpoon, W.P. Pistol Crossbow, Survival (Plains/Arctic/Desert/Mountains/Wilderness), Weather Sense, and other non-core skills not in the 1994 rulebook
